Growth Partners Programme

Price: £4,995 excl VAT
If you are a mamber of The Supper Club click here.

The Growth Partner Programme (GPP) is a non-academic, tools-based programme that uses the insights from hundreds of entrepreneurs to help you to drive capital value in your business. It has been designed specifically for busy business owners who are keen to avoid the common pitfalls of growth. GPP will help you to drive efficiencies and increase margins in your business, with the aim of increasing capital value by at least 20%.

The small groups of business owners provide an opportunity to learn in a supportive environment, and to gain fresh perspective on your business from your peers.

Each group has access to one-on-one coaching throughout the programme, as well as a private LinkedIn group for participants to share knowledge and to support each other.

GPP is driven by The Supper Club, a membership club exclusively for high-growth entrepreneurs.

Is it right for you? If you relate to one or more of the following in your business then you might want to consider GPP:

  • Your business growth is limited yet you’ve never been busier
  • Your resources are constantly stretched
  • You want a sustainable business that’s not reliant on you for growth

The programme is delivered over four full days across three months - after each session you apply the learnings to your business.

  • Day 1: Grand Design
    Identify your business goals and the routes to achieve them

  • Day 2: Pole Position
    Position your business for success within a competitive landscape
  • Day 3: Ultimate Impact
    Measure and refine internal and external communications to maximise performance

  • Day 4: Sustainable Success
    Recognise and mitigate risks ​and build the right team to deliver on business goals

Maximize Capital Value

Price: £795 excl VAT
If you are a mamber of The Supper Club click here.

This one-day Master Class has been developed by The Supper Club’s founder Duncan Cheatle in partnership with Cavendish, the UK’s leading Corporate Finance boutique. It is aimed at owner-managers of fast-growing businesses who want to plan now to maximise the value on the sale of their business in the future, typically in two to six years.

This Master Class will leave you in no doubt about how to maximise the value of your business. You will leave the programme with the knowledge, tools and an action plan that will ensure a higher valuation of your company when you decide to sell.

Is it right for you? This Master Class is for you if:

  • you are thinking about exiting or selling your business in the next two to six years
  • you want to drive up capital value in preparation for an eventual sale

Bespoke Programmes

Enterprise-driven, we work with organisations to develop bespoke programmes to meet their needs in the entrepreneurial space. We work with the organisations who are helping entrepreneurs and their teams to scale their businesses.

To date, we have run over 1,500 round table discussions with members of The Supper Club about business challenges and growth strategies. We are therefore in a privileged position of understanding high-growth businesses and the people who run them like no-one else we know. Our bespoke programmes harness these years of entrepreneurial experience from hundreds of successful entrepreneurs.

If you would like to discuss your needs and how we may be able to help you achieve them, please get in touch.

Frequently Asked Questiones

I’m a member of The Supper Club. Do I get a different rate?
Yes, members of The Supper Club get the special rate of £3995 excl VAT.
How long is each day?
Each day of GPP is a full day, from 8am – 6pm
Who else will be in my group?
Each GPP group is capped at 15, ensuring a small and supportive group. The group will be made up of other business owners looking to grow their business, and will be very cross-sector.
I’ve completed GPP but have a question about my business. Can I speak to someone?
It depends on what you’ve done. The best thing is to give us a ring and we would be happy to talk through your circumstances and see what is right for you.
I’ve recently done some coaching/training. Should I still join GPP?
Of course, give us a ring or fill out the contact form and we would be happy to see what we can do.
